🧠 Brainmash Guide

Welcome to Brainmash – the absurd creativity engine. It’s fast. It’s weird. It might be haunted. Let’s show you how to break it (creatively, of course).

✨ What is Brainmash?

It’s a static, no-login idea generator that fuses your brain’s weirdest impulses with twisted logic. Drop in 3 unrelated concepts and let the engine generate bizarre combinations, alternate universes, fake product ideas, or meme-level plot twists.

🛠️ How to Use

  1. Type any 3 ideas (words, themes, moods, etc.).
  2. Optionally choose an idea style (e.g., Startup, Myth, Meme...)
  3. Click Generate. Prepare for the absurd.
  4. Click Remix to scramble it again (up to 5 times).
  5. Read. Laugh. Share. Or cry, depending on the result.

🌀 Template Packs

We’ve got flavor modes:

  • Fusion: merge chaos into a thing
  • Disruption: logic-breaking twists
  • Snapshot: alt-universe views
  • Meme, Sci-Fi, Myth, Product, Startup and more

🥠 Fortune Fusion Mode

If your input includes dark or existential vibes (e.g. “crisis”, “emptiness”, “failure”), Brainmash syncs with MisfortuneCookie.top and injects a perfectly rude cookie into your output.

⚠️ Disclaimer

This tool is powered by AI-adjacent logic and satire. It’s meant for creativity, brainstorming, or just making weird stuff. Don’t take its outputs too literally (or try to sue us, please).
